Simple apple pie

Source: Nami-Nami
Ingredients Amount
Wheat flour 240g (4dl)
Sugar 125g (1,5dl)
Vanilla sugar 2 teaspoon (if wanted)
Baking powder 1,5 teaspoons
salt a pinch
Butter 100g
Eggs 2 large
Kefir 2dl
Apple 500g
Cinnamon or brown sugar as you like :)
  1. Wash the apples, remove the cores and cut the apples into small cubes (if you use store-bought apples, you can peel them first). Mix the dry ingredients together.
  2. Melt the butter, put it in the dough bowl. Add the sour milk little by little, then beat in the eggs. Add dry ingredients and apple cubes.
  3. Put the dough in an even layer in a greased 25x32 cm cake pan (make double the amount of dough for a plate cake), sprinkle with demerara sugar and, if desired, e.g. cinnamon.
  4. Bake in a 200-degree oven until light yellow (about 40-45 minutes). If desired, sprinkle powdered sugar on the slightly cooled cake.
